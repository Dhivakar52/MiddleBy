Tim FitzGerald

CEO & Chairman

06/28/2025
Tim Fitzgerald serves as Chief Executive Officer of The Middleby Corporation, a global leader in commercial foodservice, residential kitchen, and food processing equipment. With a strong financial and strategic background, Tim has played a key role in advancing Middleby's innovation, global expansion, and operational excellence. Under his leadership, the company continues to invest in cutting-edge technology and customer-focused solutions. Tim is committed to driving sustainable growth and fostering a high-performance culture across Middleby’s portfolio of brands.
