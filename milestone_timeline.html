Middleby Milestones

Smart Kitchen Innovation Launch
In Q1 2025, Middleby will strengthen its extant smart kitchen innovations, expanding IoT-enabled solutions to drive greater efficiency and sustainability in commercial kitchens.

Pilot Automation Program 2.0
During Q2 2025, Middleby advances its extant automation program, introducing version 2.0 that leverages robotics and AI to enhance global manufacturing processes.

AI Predictive Cooking
In January 2022, Middleby enhanced its extant AI capabilities by releasing predictive cooking algorithms. These innovations improved accuracy in meal preparation, helping chefs maintain consistency. The extant system also reduced food waste, ensuring sustainable and efficient operations.

Smart Vending Solutions
By May 2022, Middleby expanded its extant smart vending technologies with IoT-enabled, fully automated food delivery units. These extant solutions provided customers with convenient, contactless access to meals anytime. They also optimized operations for businesses by reducing manual oversight.
